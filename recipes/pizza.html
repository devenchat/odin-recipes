Pizza

Description

Homemade pizza with a crisp crust, flavorful tomato sauce, melty cheese, and your choice of toppings. This easy, classic recipe is great for family meals and can be customized for any taste.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast (1 packet)
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water (105–110°F)
  • 3 1/2 to 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 cup pizza sauce or marinara sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Favorite toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell pepper, onions, olives, basil etc.)

Directions

  1. In a bowl, combine warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let sit 5-10 minutes until foamy.
  2. Stir in 2 tablespoons olive oil, salt, and 3 1/2 cups flour gradually until dough forms. Add more flour if needed until dough is soft and slightly tacky.
  3. Turn dough onto lightly floured surface and knead 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place dough in oiled bowl, cover, and let rise in warm place 1 hour or until doubled.
  5. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C). If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven while preheating.
  6. Punch down dough and divide into 2 balls (for medium pizzas). Roll out each on floured surface to desired thickness.
  7. Transfer dough to a sheet pan or pizza peel. Spread sauce, sprinkle cheese, and add toppings.
  8. Bake 10-14 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly. Cool 2 minutes, slice, and serve.
