Butter Chicken

Description

An easy butter chicken recipe I've perfected over the years and shared with many others. The sauce is perfect when creamy and not too thick or too thin. The level of salt and spices can be suited to your taste. Garam and tandoori masala can be found in the ethnic aisle of your grocery store.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, divided
  • 1 onion, minced
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 3 cups heavy cream
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 1/2 pounds skinless, boneless, chicken breast
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons tandoori masala

Directions

  1. Gather ingredients, preheat oven to 375 F
  2. Melt 2 tablespoons butter in skillet over medium heat, stir in onion and garlic
  3. Combine cream, tomato sauce, butter, salt, cayenne pepper, garam masala in pan over medium
  4. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer, stirring for 30 minutes
  5. Toss chicken with vegetable oil until coated. Season with tandoori masala
  6. Bake chicken in preheated oven for 12 minutes
  7. Add cooked chicken to sauce and simmer for 5 minutes
