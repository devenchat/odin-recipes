French Fries

Description

Classic oven-baked or pan-fried french fries that are crispy on the outside and tender inside. Seasoned simply with salt and optional herbs, these fries are easy to customize and a perfect side dish or snack.

Ingredients

  • 4 large russet potatoes
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil (or olive oil)
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt, plus more to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika (optional)
  • Fresh parsley, finely chopped, for garnish (optional)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Wash and peel potatoes (if desired). Cut into even sticks, about 1/4 to 1/2 inch thick.
  3. Soak cut potatoes in cold water for 30 minutes to remove excess starch, then drain and pat dry thoroughly.
  4. In a large bowl, toss potatoes with oil, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika until evenly coated.
  5. Spread fries in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet without overcrowding.
  6. Bake for 20 minutes, flip fries, then bake another 15-20 minutes until golden brown and crispy.
  7. Remove from oven, season with additional salt if needed, and garnish with parsley before serving.
